Compact + Enhance

Two independent toggles that compose together. Compact rewrites verbose corporate phrasing into tighter text using pattern-matched rules. Enhance adds visual highlights. Both preserve the original — toggle off to revert.

Compact Rules Engine

Pattern-matched phrase replacements. Runs on the plain text extracted from the body HTML before enhance is applied.

Category Matches Becomes
Throat-clearing Please be advised that, We wanted to make you aware that, We are pleased to inform you that (removed)
Filler phrases at this point in time, as part of our ongoing efforts to, in order to now, (removed), to
Passive → Active you may experience, will be migrating, are currently affected expect, migrating, affected
Wordy constructions Failure to do so will result in, please don't hesitate to, We appreciate your patience and cooperation Otherwise:, (removed), (removed)
Redundant qualifiers approximately, any and all, each and every, under any circumstances ~, all, every, (removed)
Sentence merging ...service. This issue began at... ...service since [time].
Trailing padding We will provide updates as more information becomes available. (removed — implied by alert system)
