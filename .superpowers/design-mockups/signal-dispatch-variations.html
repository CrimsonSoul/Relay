Variation A2
Phosphor Terminal
The same data-first philosophy but through a warm CRT phosphor lens. Green glow on deep black, faint scanlines, and a blinking cursor. Nostalgic but not kitschy — the information density stays high.

  • CRT scanline overlay
  • Blinking cursor in header
  • Two-line entry layout
  • Numbered entries (hex-style)
  • Warm amber secondary accent
  • Inline field labels
