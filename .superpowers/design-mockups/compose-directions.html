Relay Compose Tab

Three design directions that move away from the current dark+amber template feel. Each explores a different personality while keeping the same functionality: group selection, recipient list, and action toolbar.

Direction A
Signal Dispatch
High information density. Every pixel earns its place. Monospaced typography creates a utilitarian, operations-center feel. Red signal color replaces amber for urgency without warmth.
  • IBM Plex Mono throughout
  • Table rows, not cards
  • Signal red accent (#E11D48)
  • Tight, data-dense layout
  • Status indicators per contact
  • Bloomberg / ops-center energy

Direction B
Warm Analog
Rich textures and serif typography evoke craft and care. Card-based layout with warm earth tones replaces the cold dark+amber formula. Feels like a curated address book, not a spreadsheet.
  • Fraunces serif + Instrument Sans
  • Card grid, not rows
  • Warm earth palette (#C2956B)
  • Noise texture overlay
  • Rounded, generous spacing
  • Stationery / leather notebook feel

Direction C
Kinetic Grid
Bold geometric shapes and high-energy typography. A collapsed icon rail frees up space. Indigo accent feels modern without being cold. Chunky contact rows with squared avatars break the card monotony.
  • Syne display + Archivo body
  • Icon rail instead of wide sidebar
  • Indigo accent (#6366F1)
  • Squared avatars, chunky rows
  • Bold weight hierarchy
  • Bauhaus / dashboard energy
